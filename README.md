# UK home energy calculators — greenhomesums.co.uk

Free, data-driven calculators for UK homeowners and landlords making home energy decisions. All tools use live Ofgem price cap data and are updated each quarter.

## Calculators

### Solar
- [Solar panel savings calculator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/solar-panel-savings-uk) — are solar panels worth it? Personalised cost, savings and payback by postcode. Powered by PVGIS satellite irradiance data.
- [Solar panel payback calculator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/solar-payback-by-postcode-uk) — payback period and 25-year savings by UK postcode and region.
- [Solar battery storage UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/solar-battery-ev-combined-savings-uk) — combined savings from solar panels, home battery and EV charging. Cost, payback and 15-year net benefit.
- [Solar export tariff comparator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/solar-export-tariff-comparator-uk) — which UK supplier pays most for your exported solar electricity (SEG rates).

### Heat pumps
- [Air source heat pump cost UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/heat-pump-vs-gas-boiler-uk) — install cost, running cost vs gas boiler, and 15-year total cost of ownership with BUS grant.
- [Boiler Upgrade Scheme grant calculator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/boiler-upgrade-scheme-grant-uk) — check BUS eligibility and net install cost after the £7,500 grant.
- [Heat loss calculator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/heat-loss-calculator-uk) — heat pump sizing by property type, insulation level and room-by-room heat demand.

### Battery storage
- [Home battery storage UK without solar](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/home-battery-payback-uk) — standalone battery payback on Octopus Flux, Cosy or Go. Capacity sizing and 10-year net.

### Insulation
- [Loft and cavity wall insulation cost UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/insulation-savings-calculator-uk) — annual savings, payback and ECO4 grant impact for loft, cavity wall and floor insulation.

### EPC and landlords
- [EPC band upgrade cost calculator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/epc-band-upgrade-cost-uk) — cheapest measure stack and total cost to reach a target EPC band.
- [MEES landlord compliance calculator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/mees-landlord-compliance-uk) — cheapest path to EPC C by 2028, grant eligibility and penalty exposure.
- [Green mortgage savings calculator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/green-mortgage-savings-uk) — lifetime saving from a green mortgage rate discount for EPC C+ properties.

### Tariffs and EVs
- [Octopus Agile savings calculator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/octopus-agile-savings-uk) — half-hourly tariff savings vs the Ofgem flat cap.
- [Octopus Cosy vs Flux tariff comparator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/cosy-vs-flux-tariff-comparator-uk) — which Octopus tariff is cheaper for heat pump and battery households.
- [Ofgem price cap impact calculator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/ofgem-price-cap-impact-uk) — what each quarterly cap change means for your annual bill.
- [EV home charging cost calculator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/ev-home-charging-cost-uk) — cost per mile across UK time-of-use tariffs vs public charging vs petrol.
- [EV vs petrol running cost calculator UK](https://greenhomesums.co.uk/calculators/ev-vs-petrol-running-cost-uk) — 10-year running cost comparison with inflation and tariff modelling.

## Data sources

- **Ofgem price cap** — updated each quarter (January, April, July, October)
- **PVGIS** — European Commission Joint Research Centre satellite-derived solar irradiance dataset
- **MCS installer database** — heat pump SCOP benchmarks by property archetype
- **DESNZ housing stock data** — UK average heat demand by property type and insulation level
